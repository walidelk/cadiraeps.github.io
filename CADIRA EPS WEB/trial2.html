Mission

Our mission in the market is to introduce ourselves to the world of chairs for unusual use, which are capable of being easily assembled and disassembled, both for private and public use. We want to offer a lightweight chair that can be transported easily, is fully removable and can be used both indoors and outdoors occasionally. It is not a chair to be used on a day-to-day basis. Its function is for sporadic use, having it stored without taking up space and being able to assemble and disassemble it quickly, easily and effectively. Their job may be to make up for the lack of chairs in the private sphere or for acts and shows in the street, moving away from the traditional stackable plastic chairs, which are not ecological, are easily deformed and are aesthetically a model already widely implanted in bars. and terraces. Our vision is to be able to use our chairs in different shows, concerts, etc. and that both city councils and other public bodies obtain a large stock to start working with biodegradable elements such as local wood.

Objective

The objective of the business is to compete with the traditional market for the typical plastic chair for events and even restoration, The chair aims to be attractive for its design and for the use of a while on a bar terrace, a concert, etc., it can attract and differentiate itself from the traditional chair Its versatility of use and the fact that it is completely removable, allows easy storage both at home and in ships.
